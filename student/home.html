Healthy mind, healthy body, healthy you. Welcome to our comprehensive healthcare platform!

Welcome to the comprehensive healthcare platform designed for the hostellers of our college campus. Our web application is designed to make healthcare more accessible, convenient and improve the quality of medical care for our students. With this platform, you can book appointments with our on-campus doctor, upload prescriptions, order medicines, and keep track of your medical records. We understand that your health is of utmost importance, and we are committed to providing you with the support you need to thrive both physically and academically.

Physical health is the bulwark of a keen mind. To ensure that our students have access to medicines and medical advice, a well-stocked dispensary is provided in the Bio-Medical department and our campus doctor is on call to respond to our students medical needs
